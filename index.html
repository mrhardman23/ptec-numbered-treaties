PTEC's Numbered Treaty Websites

This is the landing page for the websites that were designed by Grade 10 PTEC students beginning in 2022. One website has been chosen for each of the Numbered Treaties that make up Manitoba, which include Treaty 1, Treaty 2, Treaty 3, Treaty 4, Treaty 5, and Treaty 5 Adhesion, as well as Treaties 6 and 10 due to the communities in Manitoba that are signatories to these Treaties.

Please click on the options below to learn more about each of the Numbered Treaties that are part of Manitoba and remember "We Are All Treaty People." - Treaty Relations Commission of Manitoba

NOTE: These websites are best viewed on a computer as these students did not learn how to make websites responsive for mobile devices.
